Ruth's Inspiration

Ruth witnessed many girls missing school because they could not afford sanitary towels. This reality touched her deeply because she went through the same struggle while growing up.

"When I was young, affording sanitary towels was a big deal. Sometimes I had to use toilet paper or pieces of cloth. I started this foundation so that no girl has to miss school or lose confidence because of her period."

— Ruth Kiriba, Founder
