Our Story

Ruth witnessed many girls missing school because they could not afford sanitary towels. This reality touched her deeply because she went through the same struggle while growing up. Affording sanitary towels was a big challenge, and at times she had to use toilet paper or pieces of cloth during her menstrual periods. These experiences affected her comfort, dignity, and confidence.

Seeing girls continue to suffer in silence motivated Ruth to start this foundation—to ensure that no girl is forced to miss school or lose confidence simply because of her period.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

A world where every girl child lives with dignity, confidence, and equal opportunity.

Our Mission

To empower the girl child by providing sanitary towels, menstrual health education, and confidence-building programs that enable girls to stay in school and believe in themselves.

What We Do

Sanitary Towel Donation

We distribute sanitary towels to needy girls in schools and communities to ensure they attend school comfortably during their menstrual periods.

Menstrual Health Education

We conduct menstrual hygiene education sessions to help girls understand their bodies and manage their periods safely and confidently.

Girl Child Empowerment

Through mentorship talks and confidence-building programs, we encourage girls to believe in themselves, speak up, and pursue their dreams.

Community Awareness

We work with parents, teachers, and community members to break stigma around menstruation and support the girl child.

Our Core Values

Dignity
